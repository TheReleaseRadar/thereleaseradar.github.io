Frequently Asked Questions


Q: When is the calendar updated?

A: The calendar is updated at the end of every month and each shoe has info such as where to buy and how much it will resell for.


Q:How does reselling work?

A: It is a pretty simple process, you start by finding the shoe the you want to buy, finding out the date it drops and then how much it will resell for. After that you will want to go to the website on the day that it releases and try to buy the shoe. For beginners the easiest sizes to get are any size over 11. When you get the shoe all you have left to do is sell it. There are many different places to sell them such as StockX, Goat, Ebay, and grailed.


Q: What does WTB stand for?

A: WTB stands for Where To Buy.


Q: What goes into picking Our Favorites?

A: a few different things go into picking which shoes go into Our Favorites. The design, shape, and resell price are the main factors we look at when picking Our Favorites
